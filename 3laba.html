Character Animation

Character Animation is a visually compelling work of art that conveys a profound narrative using symbols. The animation utilizes a series of symbols to tell a story, invoking emotions and conveying messages without the need for words. It's a thought-provoking and immersive experience that invites viewers to interpret the symbolism.

Idea

The car is at the start. Abruptly begins to move. The rear wheel is spinning, but the front wheel is not, because the start was very sharp. Then the car descends on both wheels and drives forward.
